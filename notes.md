## Research Notes

### Quantum Optics

#### 1. Photon Statistics
- **Bose-Einstein Distribution**: Thermal light statistics
- **Poissonian Distribution**: Coherent light statistics
- **Sub-Poissonian Distribution**: Non-classical light statistics
- **Super-Poissonian Distribution**: Bunched photon statistics

#### 2. Quantum Entanglement
- **Bell States**: Maximally entangled two-qubit states
- **GHZ States**: Multi-particle entanglement
- **W States**: Another type of multi-particle entanglement
- **Entanglement Measures**: Concurrence, entanglement entropy

#### 3. Quantum Interference
- **Hong-Ou-Mandel Effect**: Two-photon interference
- **Franson Interference**: Nonlocal quantum interference
- **Quantum Erasure**: Erasing which-path information

### Quantum Key Distribution

#### 1. QKD Protocols
- **BB84 Protocol**: First QKD protocol using polarization
- **E91 Protocol**: Entanglement-based QKD
- **SARG04 Protocol**: Robust against photon-number splitting attacks
- **CV-QKD**: Continuous-variable QKD

#### 2. High-Dimensional QKD
- **Time-Bin Encoding**: Using temporal modes
- **Frequency Encoding**: Using spectral modes
- **Orbital Angular Momentum**: Using spatial modes
- **Hyper-Entanglement**: Multiple degrees of freedom

#### 3. Security Analysis
- **Individual Attacks**: Attack on each qubit separately
- **Collective Attacks**: Attack on multiple qubits together
- **Coherent Attacks**: Most general attacks
- **Security Proofs**: Mathematical proofs of security

### Experimental Techniques

#### 1. Single-Photon Detection
- **Avalanche Photodiodes (APDs)**: Geiger mode operation
- **Superconducting Nanowire Detectors (SNSPDs)**: High efficiency
- **Transition Edge Sensors (TES)**: Photon number resolution
- **Time-Tagging**: Recording photon arrival times

#### 2. Photon Pair Generation
- **Spontaneous Parametric Down-Conversion (SPDC)**: Bulk crystals
- **Four-Wave Mixing (FWM)**: Optical fibers
- **Quantum Dots**: Deterministic sources
- **Micro-Ring Resonators**: Integrated sources

#### 3. Quantum State Characterization
- **Quantum State Tomography**: Full state reconstruction
- **Quantum Process Tomography**: Characterizing quantum channels
- **Randomized Benchmarking**: Characterizing quantum gates
- **Direct State Measurement**: Measuring specific properties

### Mathematical Methods

#### 1. Quantum Mechanics
- **Hilbert Spaces**: Mathematical framework for quantum mechanics
- **Operators**: Observables and transformations
- **Density Matrices**: Mixed state representation
- **Quantum Channels**: Open quantum systems

#### 2. Information Theory
- **Shannon Entropy**: Classical information measure
- **Von Neumann Entropy**: Quantum information measure
- **Mutual Information**: Correlation measure
- **Quantum Relative Entropy**: Distance measure

#### 3. Probability Theory
- **Bayesian Inference**: Updating probabilities
- **Maximum Likelihood Estimation**: Parameter estimation
- **Monte Carlo Methods**: Statistical sampling
- **Stochastic Processes**: Random processes
