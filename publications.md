## Research Areas

My research covers the following areas:

- **High-Dimensional Quantum Key Distribution (HD-QKD)**: Time-frequency encoding, DWDM multiplexing
- **Time-Frequency Entanglement**: JTI/JSI characterization, Schmidt number estimation
- **Quantum Interferometry**: Franson interferometry, HOM interference
- **Quantum Communication Security**: PIE/SKR analysis, finite-key bounds
